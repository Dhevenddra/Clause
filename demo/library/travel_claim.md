# CLAIM FORM — CLM-51226
Policyholder: M. Krishnan · Policy: TR-3308 · Date of loss: 28 June 2026 · Date notified: 03 July 2026

I was on a trekking holiday in Himachal (trip insured 20 June – 05 July). On 28 June,
during a guided climb to a ridge at roughly 3,400 metres, I slipped and fractured my
wrist. I was treated at a clinic in Manali the same day: X-ray, cast, and medication,
total ₹48,500 (itemised bills and the doctor's report attached).

I did not call the assistance line at the time as there was no mobile coverage on the
mountain and I was in pain; I notified them after returning home on 3 July. I have no
adventure sports endorsement on my schedule — the agent told me trekking does not
count as mountaineering. I also lost my sunglasses (₹12,000) somewhere on the descent
and wish to include them in the claim.
