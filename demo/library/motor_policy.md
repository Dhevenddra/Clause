# ORBIT MOTOR PRIVATE VEHICLE POLICY — MV-5521 (Comprehensive)

## Section 1 — Coverage
1.1 This policy covers loss of or damage to the insured vehicle caused by accident, fire, lightning, external explosion, theft, riot, strike, storm, flood, or malicious act by a third party.
1.2 The insured vehicle is covered up to its Insured Declared Value (IDV) of ₹9,40,000.
1.3 Factory-fitted accessories are covered within the IDV; non-factory accessories are covered up to ₹30,000 in aggregate only if declared at inception.
1.4 Towing and roadside recovery costs following a covered event are reimbursed up to ₹7,500 per event.
1.5 Personal accident cover for the registered owner-driver applies up to ₹15,00,000 while travelling in, mounting, or dismounting the vehicle.

## Section 2 — Exclusions
2.1 Loss or damage occurring while the vehicle is driven by any person not holding a valid driving licence is excluded.
2.2 Loss or damage occurring while the vehicle is used for hire, reward, racing, pace-making, or speed testing is excluded.
2.3 Mechanical or electrical breakdown, wear and tear, and depreciation are excluded.
2.4 Damage to tyres and tubes alone is excluded unless the vehicle is damaged in the same event, in which case liability is limited to 50% of the replacement cost.
2.5 Loss or damage attributable to driving under the influence of intoxicating liquor or drugs is excluded.
2.6 Consequential loss of any kind, including loss of use and diminution in market value, is excluded.

## Section 3 — Conditions
3.1 Any accident, loss, or damage must be reported to the insurer within 48 hours of its occurrence or discovery.
3.2 Theft of the vehicle must additionally be reported to the police immediately, and a copy of the FIR provided within 7 days.
3.3 The vehicle must hold a valid certificate of fitness and registration at the time of loss.
3.4 A compulsory deductible of ₹2,500 applies to each claim; an additional voluntary deductible of ₹5,000 applies as endorsed.
3.5 Repairs may not commence before a survey is completed, except emergency repairs up to ₹15,000 with itemised bills.
3.6 The insurer's liability for repairs at a non-authorised garage is limited to the assessed amount, payable on submission of original invoices.
