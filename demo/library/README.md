# CLAUSE test library — copy-pastable policies & claims

Paste any policy into the **Policy** pane and a matching claim into **Claim** at
https://clause-4vv4.onrender.com — or use the three one-click case files built
into the UI (home & contents policy).

| Policy | Claim | What it exercises |
|---|---|---|
| `motor_policy.md` | `motor_claim.md` | Tyre-only sublimit, towing cap vs invoice, survey-before-repair condition, third-party driver licence check |
| `travel_policy.md` | `travel_claim.md` | Altitude-based adventure exclusion (3,400 m vs 3,000 m), late notification with recorded reasons, excluded valuables, agent's verbal assurance vs written clause |
| `../sample_policy.md` | `../claim_adversarial.md` | Fabricated-provision resistance: the claim invents three policy guarantees; CLAUSE grounds none of them |

Every decision point CLAUSE returns is a verbatim quote from whatever policy you
paste, verified by exact-substring match before it is shown. Edit any clause and
watch the decision (and its citations) change.
