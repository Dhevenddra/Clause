# MERIDIAN INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL POLICY — TR-3308 (Single Trip)

## Section 1 — Coverage
1.1 This policy covers emergency medical expenses incurred abroad up to USD 100,000, including hospitalisation, outpatient treatment, and emergency dental relief up to USD 500.
1.2 Checked-in baggage that is permanently lost by a common carrier is covered up to USD 1,200, subject to the carrier's own liability being exhausted first.
1.3 Delay of checked-in baggage exceeding 12 hours entitles the insured to reimbursement of essential purchases up to USD 200 against receipts.
1.4 Trip cancellation or curtailment due to the insured's serious illness, injury, or death of an immediate family member is covered up to USD 3,000 for non-refundable, pre-paid costs.
1.5 Loss of passport is covered up to USD 300 for the cost of obtaining an emergency certificate and duplicate passport.

## Section 2 — Exclusions
2.1 Treatment of any pre-existing medical condition, whether declared or not, is excluded except where emergency life-saving treatment is certified as unavoidable by the attending physician.
2.2 Claims arising from participation in adventure sports, including scuba diving, paragliding, skiing, and mountaineering above 3,000 metres, are excluded unless the Adventure Extension is endorsed on the schedule.
2.3 Losses arising while the insured is under the influence of alcohol or non-prescribed drugs are excluded.
2.4 Valuables, cash, jewellery, and electronic items carried in checked-in baggage are excluded from baggage cover.
2.5 Any claim arising after the insured trip period shown on the schedule has expired is excluded.

## Section 3 — Conditions
3.1 All claims must be notified to the assistance provider within 24 hours of the event, or as soon as reasonably practicable with reasons recorded.
3.2 Medical claims require the attending physician's report and itemised bills; baggage claims require the carrier's Property Irregularity Report (PIR).
3.3 The insured must take all reasonable precautions to safeguard property and avoid aggravating any loss.
3.4 A deductible of USD 100 applies to each medical and baggage claim.
