# CLAIM FORM — CLM-88231
Policyholder: G. Meenakshi · Policy: HP-2214 · Date of loss: 02 July 2026 · Date notified: 05 July 2026

During the storm on the night of 2 July, a neem tree in the neighbouring compound fell onto the north side of our roof, breaking tiles and rafters. Rainwater entered through the broken roof overnight and damaged the living room ceiling, a sofa set, and a television.

Itemised losses: roof repair quotation ₹1,85,000; sofa set ₹62,000; television ₹48,000; ceiling repainting ₹22,000. Total claimed: ₹3,17,000.

The dwelling was occupied at the time. Photographs of the fallen tree and the broken roof are attached, along with the repair quotation from Sri Balaji Constructions. We covered the roof opening with a tarpaulin the next morning to prevent further water entry.
