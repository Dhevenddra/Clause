# CLAIM FORM — CLM-88347
Policyholder: S. Aravindan · Policy: HP-2214 · Date of loss: ongoing, first noticed January 2026 · Date notified: 06 July 2026

Over the past several months the wooden flooring and skirting in two bedrooms have progressively warped, blackened, and crumbled. A carpenter inspected the rooms in June and confirmed extensive damp rot and termite infestation spreading from the bathroom wall, likely developing over one to two years due to a slow seepage.

We are claiming full replacement of flooring and skirting in both rooms, plus fumigation: ₹2,64,000 in total, quotation attached. No sudden incident occurred; the damage emerged gradually and we notified as soon as the carpenter confirmed how serious it had become.
