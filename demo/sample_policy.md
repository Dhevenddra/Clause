# HORIZON HOME & CONTENTS POLICY — HP-2214 (Standard Form)

## Section 1 — Coverage
1.1 This policy covers direct physical loss or damage to the insured dwelling and its contents caused by fire, lightning, storm, flood ingress through the roof, burst internal water pipes, theft following forcible entry, and impact by vehicles or falling trees.
1.2 Contents are covered up to a combined limit of ₹8,00,000 per event. Any single item of jewellery, electronics, or art is covered up to ₹75,000 unless individually scheduled in Annexure B.
1.3 Temporary accommodation costs are covered up to ₹15,000 per week for a maximum of eight weeks if the dwelling is rendered uninhabitable by an insured event.

## Section 2 — Exclusions
2.1 Loss or damage caused by gradual deterioration, wear and tear, rot, mould, or infestation is excluded.
2.2 Theft is excluded where there is no evidence of forcible or violent entry to the dwelling.
2.3 Loss or damage occurring while the dwelling has been unoccupied for more than 60 consecutive days is excluded.
2.4 Damage caused by or arising from commercial activity conducted at the dwelling is excluded unless declared and endorsed.
2.5 Flood damage to contents kept in a basement is excluded unless stored at least 30 centimetres above floor level.

## Section 3 — Conditions
3.1 The policyholder must notify the insurer of any claim within 14 days of discovering the loss.
3.2 A First Information Report (FIR) or police acknowledgment is required for all theft claims.
3.3 The policyholder must take reasonable steps to prevent further loss after an incident.
3.4 The excess (deductible) is ₹10,000 per claim event.
3.5 Fraudulent or materially exaggerated claims void this policy in full.
